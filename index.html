Origin

Memphis Roots,
Texas Edge

Born in Memphis. Not the postcards — the version with two-tone Buicks idling outside Gus's chicken and the Redbirds grinding under the lights at AutoZone Park. That city teaches you to find beauty in places nobody's marketing.

Moved to Texas. Marble Falls first, then the orbit of Austin — where Augie Garrido built a program that won national titles by trusting process over pedigree. That philosophy stuck. You prepare, you respond, you compete with yourself.

BSI grew from there: the conviction that meaningful sports analytics shouldn't require an ESPN credential or a Bloomberg terminal. That the fan checking scores at midnight deserves the same depth as the scout with the radar gun.
