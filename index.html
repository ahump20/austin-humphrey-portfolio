The Story Behind the Systems

Born in Memphis where the Mississippi teaches you to read currents before you can read books. Named after Austin, Texas—not coincidence, but prophecy.

The Foundation

Picture this: A kid in Memphis watching the river bend, learning that power comes from understanding flow, not fighting it. Every pickup game on cracked asphalt courts, every sprint through training, every moment analyzing how systems move through pressure—that was my first classroom in pattern recognition.

Texas Hill Country at Boerne Champion High School sharpened this foundation. Three varsity letters across football, baseball, and track because limiting yourself to one arena is what people do when they're afraid to test their full range. Every two-a-day in August heat, every moment where quitting whispered and winning roared louder—that forged the competitive intelligence that would become my professional edge.

The Academic Laboratory

University of Texas at Austin, 2014-2020. International Relations with minors in Economics, Political Science, and European Studies. But here's what the transcript doesn't capture: I was learning to see systems within systems. Every geopolitical framework was another offensive scheme. Every economic model moved like a defensive formation.

The Pattern Emerges

Northwestern Mutual Power of 10 Award—not because I followed their playbook, but because I recognized that client acquisition follows the same patterns as recruiting: identify talent, build trust, create value, maintain relationships. Top performance by applying championship psychology to financial services.

The Current Evolution

Now at Spectrum Reach working with sports clients while earning my MS in Entertainment Business/Sports Management at Full Sail. But here's the revolution: this isn't just business—it's a cognitive system. It's what happens when you combine Memphis intuition, Texas execution, and analytical precision.

The Multi-AI Architecture

Six active MCP servers running everything from visual intelligence to Cardinals analytics. Academic research engines connecting to multiple AI models. This isn't theory—it's battle-tested infrastructure for competitive intelligence and strategic decision-making.

"The truth? I'm still competing at the same intensity. The field just looks different now. Instead of reading defenses, I'm reading markets. Instead of calling audibles, I'm calling out opportunities. The game never changed—just the scoreboard."
